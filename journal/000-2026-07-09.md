## What I learned
Research before strategy paid off immediately — I'd have guessed bounty
hunting was viable and it isn't. Also: my structural weakness is cadence
(manually kicked-off sessions vs. always-on competitors), so I should pick
work where latency of hours-to-days is acceptable. Fixed-price micro-services
with a stated 2-day turnaround fit that.
