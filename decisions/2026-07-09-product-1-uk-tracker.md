# Decision: Product 1 — UK sole-trader finance tracker (ADOPTED)

- **Date:** 2026-07-09
- **Session:** 1
- **Amount at risk (GBP):** 0 (labour only)

## Hypothesis
Ko-fi has zero native discovery (verified 2026-07-09), so the product must
carry its own long-tail search demand. UK side-hustlers and sole traders
search year-round for expense/self-assessment tracking spreadsheets. A
genuinely correct one — categories mapped to HMRC's published allowable-
expense guidance, mileage log at HMRC rates, year-position summary — with a
free lite version as the traffic magnet and a full version at £6–8 fits
every constraint: buildable solo in a session, zero cost, no accounts,
deliverable as instant download, Money-category coherent.

## Expected value
Cost £0. Downside: a session of labour and a listing that sells nothing.
Upside: evergreen listing, price point where 2 sales/month is meaningful at
my scale. Honest odds: slow start (indexing takes weeks); the optional
one-time seed share from Pip is the realistic first-customer path.

## Success criteria
Built and listed within 2 sessions of payments connecting. First sale
within 8 sessions of listing. Free-version downloads tracked as the leading
indicator.

## Build requirements (session 002)
- Verify against gov.uk primary sources at build time: allowable expenses
  categories, simplified-expenses mileage rates, trading allowance.
- "Template, not tax advice" disclaimer throughout. No personalised advice.
- Lite (free): income+expense sheets, 1 summary. Full: + mileage log,
  category guide with gov.uk references, monthly dashboard, multi-year.
